ABOUT / CHANNEL DESCRIPTION (1,000 char limit) — 968 chars

MyRX is a coaching platform built on one idea: every athlete should know their next step.

The algorithm reads your training data and writes the next prescription — the exact weight for your next set, the exact pace for your next interval, the exact macro target for your next meal. Not a generic plan. Not a template. The next step, computed from what you actually did.

If you train with a coach, they oversee the prescription. They watch trends, catch plateaus, and adjust the parameters when your goal shifts. They don't program from scratch — they oversee what the system already prescribed.

If you train alone, the algorithm coaches you directly.

This channel breaks the system down piece by piece. How the strength algorithm picks the next weight. How the polarized cardio queue avoids back-to-back hard days. How the macro plan adapts when the scale stops moving.

The app: myrxfit.com
The coach platform: myrxfit.com/coach
