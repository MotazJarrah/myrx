YOUTUBE CHANNEL LINKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

YouTube supports up to 5 visible links on the channel banner overlay
plus unlimited links inside the About-tab description. The banner
links are what 90% of viewers see — order them by intent.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BANNER LINKS (channel header overlay — up to 5)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Order matters. The first link is the primary CTA, displayed largest.

1. myrxfit.com
   Display title: Get the app
   → The athlete-side conversion surface. Free tier covers strength,
     cardio, mobility; CoreRX and FullRX one-time upgrades sit
     behind one CTA. Mobile-first landing that detects platform and
     serves the right store link.

2. myrxfit.com/coach
   Display title: Become a coach
   → The B2B conversion surface. 14-day trial, three tier prices
     ($19 / $39 / $99 per month), client invite flow walkthrough.

3. myrxfit.com/how-it-works
   Display title: How MyRX picks your next step
   → The methodology page. The science behind the algorithm — Epley
     and Brzycki for strength projections, Daniels and Seiler for

     coach reads before signing up.

4. myrxfit.com/pricing
   Display title: Pricing
   → Both pricing tables (athlete and coach) on one page.

5. linkedin.com/in/motazjarrah
   Display title: Founder on LinkedIn
   → The builder-in-public surface. Coaches researching the company
     check the founder's LinkedIn before paying $99/month — give
     them the link.

VIDEO DESCRIPTION LINK BLOCK (paste at the bottom of every video)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YouTube indexes the first 200 chars of a video description AND
treats the link block as a navigational signal. Keep the block
identical across every video so the algorithm reads it as channel-
level metadata, not video-specific.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
LINK STRATEGY NOTES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

- No Linktree. YouTube's native 5-link banner replaces it. Linktree
  adds a third-party hop that breaks channel coherence and fragments
  analytics across two domains.

- Rotate link 1 (the primary CTA) for major drops:
  - Long-form video launch → swap link 1 to the YouTube watch URL
    for 7 days, then back to myrxfit.com.
  - Coach pricing change → swap link 1 to /coach/pricing for the
    launch week.
  - Always return to myrxfit.com within 14 days. The primary slot
    is a conversion surface, not a content surface.

- The "Founder on LinkedIn" slot is permanent. It serves the high-
  intent coach segment that researches the company before signing up.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VOICE-RULE COMPLIANCE ON LINK LABELS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Every label names what's on the other side. Verb-first where there's
an action, plain noun where it's reference content. No exclamation
points. No "Click here", "Learn more", "Sign up now", "Join us".

Banned label patterns avoided:
- "Click here", "Learn more", "Tap to download"
- "Sign up now!", "Join the journey", "Unlock your potential"
- "Welcome to MyRX", "Discover", "Find out"
- Emoji-leading labels ("🔥 Get the app", "👉 Coach signup")
