LINKEDIN — LINK STRATEGY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

LinkedIn personal profiles support:
- Up to 3 website links in the Contact Info section (each with a custom label)
- 1 "Featured" link at the top of the profile (post, doc, or external URL)
- Unlimited links inside posts and the About section

Below is the recommended link slate.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PRIMARY: CONTACT INFO LINKS (3 slots)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Slot 1 — Label: "Coach Platform"
URL: https://myrxfit.com/coach
Why: Highest-intent destination for the LinkedIn audience. Independent
     coaches researching MyRX land here, see pricing + how the algorithm
     handles their roster, and start a trial.

Slot 2 — Label: "Athlete App"
URL: https://myrxfit.com
Why: Coaches evaluating the platform want to see the athlete-facing
     surface their clients will use. This is the secondary landing.

Slot 3 — Label: "How MyRX Picks Your Next Step"
URL: https://myrxfit.com/about/how-it-works
Why: The methodology page. Sends the evidence-based reader to the math
     before the pricing. Highest-trust entry for the Stronger by Science
     / Galpin / Nuckols-axis coach.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FEATURED LINK (1 slot, top-of-profile pinned post or external URL)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Default: pin the launch post (see first_post_caption.txt).
After 30 days: rotate to the highest-performing carousel.
After 90 days: pin a LinkedIn-native article or a YouTube long-form
              that the coach audience has watched in volume.

The Featured slot rotates; the Contact Info links stay static.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
INSIDE THE ABOUT SECTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The About already closes with two plain-text URLs:
- myrxfit.com/coach
- myrxfit.com

LinkedIn doesn't hyperlink these automatically. That's fine — readers
who reach the bottom of the About are high-intent and will type.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHY NO LINKTREE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

LinkedIn's three-link slot system replaces the need for a Linktree-style
aggregator on this platform. Linktree fits Instagram (1 bio link) and
TikTok (1 bio link) where the single-link cap forces aggregation. On
LinkedIn, sending coaches to a third-party redirect adds a step without
adding signal — the platform already supports labeled multi-link
contact info.

If a Linktree is ever added (for cross-platform consistency), the four
links it would carry are:
1. Coach Platform → myrxfit.com/coach
2. Athlete App → myrxfit.com
3. How MyRX picks your next step → myrxfit.com/about/how-it-works
4. Latest from MyRX on YouTube → youtube.com/@teammyrx

Voice on every label stays coach-led — no "click here," no "check this
out," no emojis.
