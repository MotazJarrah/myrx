# MyRX marketing launch kit

This folder is the external-presence kit for MyRX's launch. The order below is the order to read on launch day — top to bottom, no skipping.

---

## What's in here

**Strategy + research (read once, reference later)**
- [CHANNEL_STRATEGY.md](./CHANNEL_STRATEGY.md) — the four Tier 1 channels (LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, X), the positioning slot MyRX occupies, and why each channel earns the founder's time.
- [USERNAMES_RESEARCH.md](./USERNAMES_RESEARCH.md) — handle availability across every platform. Primary is `@teammyrx`. Fallback is `@myrxapp`. `@myrxfit` is taken on Instagram, LinkedIn, and Pinterest by a competitor — don't try to claim it.
- [VOICE_CHEAT_SHEET.md](./VOICE_CHEAT_SHEET.md) — the three-pillar coach voice (acknowledge, mechanism, next step), the seven rules, the banned phrases. Re-read this every morning of the first launch week.

**Execution (use day-of and ongoing)**
- [LAUNCH_PLAYBOOK.md](./LAUNCH_PLAYBOOK.md) — step-by-step day-of execution. What to do at hour 0, hour 4, hour 24, day 3, day 7.
- [CONTENT_CALENDAR.md](./CONTENT_CALENDAR.md) — the first 30 days of posts mapped to channels, with timing and theme rotation.
- [POST_TEMPLATES.md](./POST_TEMPLATES.md) — reusable post structures for the recurring formats: client wins, science breakdowns, coach lessons, product updates.

**Per-channel assets**
- [PROFILE_KIT/](./PROFILE_KIT/) — one folder per Tier 1 channel. Each contains the bio, link-in-bio destination, first-post caption, profile picture, banner, and first-post image sized to that platform's current specs.
- [PROFILE_KIT/_VISUALS_README.md](./PROFILE_KIT/_VISUALS_README.md) — what the placeholder visuals represent and what to swap in before launch.

**Reference only**
- [_research/](./_research/) — raw research that informed the strategy (channel benchmarks, username scans, spec sheets, hashtag pools, competitive voice analysis, timing data). You won't need these on launch day; they're here for when the strategy needs updating.
- [_qa_report.md](./_qa_report.md) — the internal QA pass on this kit. Issues caught, fixes applied, remaining gaps.

---

## Read this in order on launch day

1. **VOICE_CHEAT_SHEET.md** — internalize the coach voice before you type a single word. The three pillars and the banned phrases need to be live in your head, not bookmarked.
2. **USERNAMES_RESEARCH.md** — confirm `@teammyrx` is still available on all four platforms. If one slipped overnight, the document lists the fallback order.
3. **CHANNEL_STRATEGY.md** — re-confirm the four Tier 1 channels and the order you'll set them up in. LinkedIn first (the coach segment lives there), then Instagram, then YouTube, then X.
4. **LAUNCH_PLAYBOOK.md** — step-by-step from there. The playbook tells you which `PROFILE_KIT/<channel>/` folder to open at each step.

**During execution**, the playbook routes you into the per-channel folders. Each folder's README tells you which files to copy where.

**Day 2 and beyond**, switch to CONTENT_CALENDAR.md for what to post, and POST_TEMPLATES.md for the structure of each post.

## Brand + voice quick reference

The palette is locked: dark `#131A17`, surface `#191F1C`, lime `#CAF240`, foreground `#F4F3EF`. Typography is Geist and Geist Mono. Icons are Lucide. The tagline is "Performance Lab."

The voice is a coach who oversees, not a coach who hypes. Every piece of copy acknowledges where the athlete or coach is, names the mechanism that makes the next step work, and ends with a concrete action. No exclamation points on platform copy. No "transform your life." No urgency theater. Lowercase "coach" and "athlete" in body copy. Brand mid-sentence in titles, verb-first. The seven rules and the banned-phrase list live in VOICE_CHEAT_SHEET.md — read them before you write.

## What this kit does not do

This kit does not create any social accounts. The founder signs up on launch day with their own credentials, on their own device, and handles 2FA and SMS verification themselves. Nothing here automates account creation, and nothing logs into any platform on your behalf.

This kit also does not include final photography or illustration. The images in `PROFILE_KIT/<channel>/` are placeholders sized to each platform's current spec — they hold the layout but need to be replaced with shipped assets before going live. The `_VISUALS_README.md` lists what each placeholder represents and what to swap in.

Hashtag pools, timing benchmarks, and competitive voice notes in `_research/` are reference material. They're not posts and shouldn't be copy-pasted into the live calendar without a human pass.
