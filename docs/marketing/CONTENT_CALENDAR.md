- **Long-form YouTube drops Thursday or Friday, never Monday.** Weekend distribution carries the watch-time signal that pulls the video into the algorithm.
- **No post on Sundays for LinkedIn, ever.** B2B engagement on LinkedIn craters on weekends.
- **Every Reel and Short opens with the primary keyword spoken aloud in the first 5 seconds.** This is the TikTok/Reels ASR ranking signal — non-negotiable per 2026 algorithm research.
- **Alt text on every IG post.** Treat it as a ranking signal, not accessibility chrome.
- **Zero emojis on platform copy.** Three-pillar voice rule.
